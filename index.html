About

Hey, I'm Malachiyah, a CGI generalist and incoming senior at Stephen F. Austin State University with a passion for using technology as a creative toolset. To me, CGI isn't just a technical skill, it's a way to bring virtually any creative vision to life.

I work primarily in Blender and Unreal Engine with a growing foundation in Maya, focusing on animation, environment art, technical art, and motion graphics. I'm driven by the intersection of creativity and technology, where artistic vision and software skills come together to make something meaningful.

Whether it's film, games, commercial work, or something in between, I'm excited to collaborate, keep growing, and bring fresh ideas to whatever comes next.
